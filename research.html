Research

The research the very important approach to development. Without doing the research nothing great will ever happen. The current world in different dimensions of life is full of a very changing situation with a tide of technology adoption.
This is a good reason that imperatively requests us to be aligned with such developmental advancement through our own studies to catch up and go with the flow.

Our organization is there to provide below services for African development.

  • Research and proposal projects conceptions.
  • Advisory and orientation on research projects.
  • Projects outcome and impact discussion and advisory provision.
  • Carry out the epidemiologic studies such descriptive studies (Case report, cases series and descriptive surveys) and analytical studies such as observational studies (cross-section, case control and cohort) and experimental studies (field and lab trials). Hence, we do all studies that fall in descriptive, diagnostic, predictive and prescriptive studies.
  • Applied health research identification of health problems priority, evaluation of the effectiveness of health programs and policies, and managing the optimal use of available resources.
  • Health service provision assessment.
  • Healthcare quality and accreditation assessment.
