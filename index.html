Our Vision, mission and values

Our Pillars

Research: Health and developmental research.
Health promotion: into the community to alleviate the diseases burden to people and to the nation economy.
Educational trainings: to professionals and other people for capacity building and development advancement.
Consultancy and Advisory: to the organizations with health and developmental services.

Benefits of partnering and working with us

All beneficiaries and partners are equally benefited from the endeavor and momentum of our organization as listed below:
  • Gain a deep dive on the poor health causal factors and root causes of their problems and related meticulous solutions.
  • Quick and proactive improvement of people’s health at the workplace and in their community.
  • Guidance on research process in various levels (Individual research, organization research, need assessment or baseline assessment, impact analysis) and the process of carrying out those studies.
  • Advisory on how you can conduct your personal research for valid methodology, accurate findings for a real data driven decisions.
  • Health promotion sessions regarding health maintenance, planned improvement, planned behavior change at the workplace and in community for sustainable development and sustainable development.
  • Transformational education approach for health professionals in a wide range from research methodology, medical statistical analysis, monitoring and evaluation, health programs management and policy analysis and development. etc.
  • Grant and health project management .
  • Knowledge and information sharing for learning together to ensure a cohesive, collaborative, and sustainable development.

African scientific health transformation

Advancing African people’s health requires well studied approaches with meticulously tailored content for a contextual based application. Therefore, the research-based decisions are the unprecedented solutions for current poor health of Africans.
In addition, achieving these results requires well trained and sufficient health professionals, technology to speed, ease the accessibility of basic and advanced health services, finally the sustainability strategy.

Our organization has invested his energy to help build the capacity of the entire African population because without closing the knowledge gap whether in health professionals or in the population at large, all decisions are doomed to fail.
