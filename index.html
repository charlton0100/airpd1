Who we Are

AIRPD is an African Institute of Research for Public Health and Development that aims to support communities and organizations to lessen the global burden of diseases through applied research and evidence-based generated solutions and public health skills sharing for African public health professionals. Hence, those operations are carried out with a strong belief in improving the quality of people's health.


Our Headquaters

We are a hybrid organization located in Bujumbura Burundi with teams that are operating on-site and others who are working on research fields and remotely in different countries of Africa.

Our Vision

To be the African leading research and health promotion organization.

Our Mission

To be the African leading research and health promotion organization.
